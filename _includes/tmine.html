Cartoon Network Gallery

Debut Solo Art Exhibition
Located in Cartoon Network Studios, Burbank, CA

I grew up watching a lot of Cartoon Network as a kid, and their
cartoons have had a great deal of influence
on my work.

This series of work, titled “Too Much Is Never Enough” features
selected works created over the past 5 years using pen and
markers. Smaller pieces took anywhere from 2-5 days to
complete while larger pieces upwards of 3 months.

…In the end, all the audience will see is the finished
product, a sheet of paper filled to the brim with various
intricate designs. To me, the beauty is in each moment filling
up the paper, starting from the bottom and working my way up.
The finished piece is just an inevitable bi-product of the
process.

Sometimes people will say that they think the piece looks
better not fully filled up. Although that may produce a more
immediate and gratifying visual experience for the observer,
over time with repeated viewing I feel like that power will
subside. I want my pieces to grow with the viewer over an
extended period of time.
